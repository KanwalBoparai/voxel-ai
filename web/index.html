Capabilities

Everything a great front desk does

A conversational agent with real tools — not a phone tree. It listens, answers honestly, and takes action.

Natural conversation

Powered by Claude. Handles objections, answers FAQs, discloses it's AI when asked, and never invents facts it wasn't given.

Real appointment booking

Checks live availability and books straight into Google Calendar — with conflict detection, not made-up slots.

Automatic CRM logging

Every call ends with a structured outcome saved to Google Sheets: booked, interested, callback, do-not-call and more.

Lead scoring

Ranks every prospect 0–100 by engagement so your team follows up with the hottest leads first.

SMS follow-ups

Automatically texts a recap, a confirmation, or a reminder after the call — timed to lift conversion.

Built-in guardrails

Stays strictly on the facts you configure, respects do-not-call immediately, and never pressures a caller.

How it works

From ring to booked in three steps

1

Configure your business

Name, industry, services, hours, FAQs, tone, and promotions live in one config file — no source code to touch.

2

The agent takes the call

Claude drives a natural conversation over the phone (Twilio or Vapi) with speech-to-text and lifelike voice.

3

Outcomes flow to your tools

Appointments hit your calendar, outcomes hit your CRM, and follow-up texts go out — all automatically.

Configurable, not hardcoded

Change the business, not the code

Business owners edit plain fields — no Python, no redeploy. The agent's persona, knowledge, and booking flow all come from configuration.

  • Company name, industry & contact details
  • Services, opening hours & booking rules
  • FAQs, promotions, greeting & AI personality
// config/business.json
{
  "business_name": "Bright Smile Dental",
  "industry": "dental clinic",
  "agent_name": "Ava",
  "tone": "warm, professional",
  "services": [ "Exams", "Whitening" ],
  "booking": { "appointment_label": "appointment" },
  "promotion": { "active": true }
}
